# Session 001 - A Call To Everlund

## Snapshot

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Party Level | 3 |
| Main Focus | Character introductions, Elaris's mission, the road to the Silent Coin Tavern, and the first fight. |

## Recap

The party arrived in Everlund after receiving silver-ink invitations from Elaris Vaunthel. Elaris spoke of strange places where ordinary life behaves incorrectly, including the abandoned Silent Coin Tavern in the Moonwood.

He asked the party to investigate. The tavern had a reputation for lights in empty windows, doors opening when no one should be inside, and animals nearby acting possessive and territorial.

Elaris arranged lodging, supplies, a carriage, and a driver named Tovin Reed. The carriage was stocked for travel and emergency investigation.

During the journey north, the party stopped at a roadside clearing. Wolves circled the camp in an unnatural pattern, behaving less like hungry animals and more like something defending a claim. The party fought them off and protected the horses.

The next day, the party reached the Silent Coin Tavern. It stood dark, old, and leaning in a clearing. A candle flickered in an upper window, something dragged across the floor inside, and the front door opened.
