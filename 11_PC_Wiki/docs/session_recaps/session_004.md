# Session 004 - The Silent Coin Falls

## Snapshot

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Party Level | 4 |
| Main Focus | Defeating the Silent Coin's final guardian, escaping the collapsing tavern, returning to Elaris, and beginning the road toward Brindleford. |
| Ending Point | The party is camped in old roadside ruins when disciplined humanoid movement begins closing in around the camp. |

## Recap

The session began in the Silent Coin basement, where the reunited Coiled Coin pulsed inside a guardian made from house-hunger, old ward-light, and the tavern's refusal to let go.

The fight was brutal. Chains and claimed ground lashed through the basement while the guardian tried to crush the party and keep the relic inside itself. Kal was caught in the guardian's power, Wazeau kept healing magic flowing, Aiaia fought beside Nugget and punished attackers with protective magic, and Bruce's ranged attacks kept cutting into the guardian until the creature finally collapsed.

When it fell, the Coiled Coin became a single warm, heavy black-gold gemstone-like relic.

Then the tavern began to collapse.

With the stairs falling apart and the house breaking inward, the party found the hidden river passage below the tavern. They worked together under pressure to find and hold the release points that opened the way out, then escaped through a muddy, root-filled passage into the night air.

Outside, Tovin was still waiting with the carriage and horses. From the party's perspective, only a few hours had passed inside the tavern. From Tovin's perspective, they had been gone for three days. He had tried to leave for help, but the road turned him back toward the tavern. Once the relic was sealed inside Elaris's reinforced case, the pressure around the area eased.

Back at Moongleam Tower, Elaris treated the party's injuries and poison. The party chose to keep the sealed case overnight rather than hand it over immediately, and Elaris accepted their caution. After a long rest, the party reached level 4.

The next morning, Elaris explained more of what he believes is happening. He thinks the Coiled Coin is one fragment of a much larger shattered power, and that places like the Silent Coin Tavern may form around old wounds in reality. He believes an old altar beneath the tavern was built to seal or stabilize one such wound, and that the tavern became dangerous when the relic and the altar began reacting against each other.

The party then handed the sealed relic to Elaris for study.

The blackened copper coin recovered from the Silent Coin was confirmed as a Resonant Remnant: a fragment-touched material that could hold enough power to enchant or improve an item. The party used it to create a +1 greatsword for Kal.

Elaris then introduced the next lead: Redmark Keep, an old ruin near Brindleford. Caravans have been attacked, supplies stolen, and reports from the road now suggest both savage raiders and an organized military presence. Tovin would not drive the main road, so the party set out on foot along a safer back-road route.

On the way, the party found red cloth markers, torn supplies, a raided caravan site, gnoll-like tracks, and disciplined boot tracks. They made camp in old ruins off the road. Wazeau spotted a gnoll attack before it reached the camp, and the party fought off the attackers. Two gnolls fled into the dark, and the party recovered 50 gp worth of mixed coin, trophies, trade scraps, and trinkets.

The party tried to continue resting, but later in the night they heard movement from several directions: boot steps, leather shifting, a twig snap, and a low whistle answered from another side of camp.

The gnolls came hungry.

These ones came arranged.

## Loot And Useful Finds

- The Coiled Coin was recovered, sealed in Elaris's case, and later turned over to him at Moongleam Tower.
- The blackened copper coin / Resonant Remnant was spent to create a +1 greatsword for Kal.
- The party recovered 50 gp worth of mixed coin, trophies, trade scraps, and trinkets after the gnoll attack.

## What The Party Knows Now

- The Silent Coin Tavern collapsed and no longer appears to function as an active haunted structure.
- Three days passed outside while the party experienced only a few hours inside the tavern.
- Elaris believes the Coiled Coin is part of a larger shattered power.
- Elaris believes old wounds in reality can affect magic, space, perception, and places around them.
- The old altar beneath the tavern may have stabilized the wound once the Coiled Coin was removed and contained.
- Resonant Remnants can be used to create or improve magic items.
- Redmark Keep and the road near Brindleford are the next active problem.
- The trouble near Brindleford appears to involve both gnoll violence and disciplined armed movement.
