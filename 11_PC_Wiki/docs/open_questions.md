# Open Questions

These are player-facing mysteries the party may want to remember.

## Silent Coin Tavern

* What remains beneath the collapsed tavern?
* What exactly was the old altar beneath the tavern built to do?
* Did removing the Coiled Coin fully stabilize the place, or only quiet it for now?

## The Coiled Coin

* What exactly is the Coiled Coin?
* Why did it turn ownership into obsession?
* Why did the old altar split it into four pieces?
* What will Elaris learn from studying it?
* Was the relic afraid before it was placed in the case?

## Elaris's Mission

* What does Elaris already know about relic fragments?
* Why did he choose this party?
* How does the Silent Coin Tavern connect to the prophecy he shared?

## Redmark Road

* What does the Red Banner want from Redmark Keep and the territory around it?
* How complete is the Red Banner's siege of Brindleford?
* Can the party reach Brindleford before another patrol carries word of them to the keep?
* Why are gnolls and the Red Banner both fighting for the same roads?
* What do the red cloth markers mean?
* How many more gnoll ambush sites lie between the party and town?
