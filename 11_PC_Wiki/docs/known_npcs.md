# Known NPCs

## Allies And Contacts

|Name|What The Party Knows|
|-|-|
|Elaris Vaunthel|A scholar in Everlund who invited the party to investigate strange places tied to old catastrophes, fallen stars, and possible relic fragments. He arranged lodging, supplies, and the carriage to the Silent Coin Tavern. After the party returned, he treated their injuries, received the sealed Coiled Coin for study, and sent them toward Brindleford and Redmark Keep.|
|Tovin Reed|Elaris's cheerful carriage driver. He waited outside the Silent Coin Tavern for three days while the party experienced only a few hours inside. He survived the strange road-looping pressure and drove the party back to Moongleam Tower after the relic was sealed.|

## Figures Encountered In The Silent Coin Tavern

|Name / Description|What The Party Knows|
|-|-|
|Copper-Tongued Host|A ghostly or supernatural host in the common room that demanded the party sit, wait, and be served. The party defeated or banished it.|
|Keyless Shade|A shadowy guardian tied to Room 7 and the phrase "Guests stay." It tried to keep the door closed and was defeated by the party.|
|Oren Blackpenny|The tavern's owner, who bought the Coiled Coin and became bound up in the house's hunger, ownership, ledgers, and hoard.|
|Silent Coin Guardian|The final guardian that formed around the reunited Coiled Coin in the basement. The party defeated it before escaping the collapsing tavern.|
