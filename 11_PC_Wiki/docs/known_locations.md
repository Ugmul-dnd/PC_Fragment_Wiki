### Arc 1 - Silent Coin Tavern

#### Everlund

The city where the party met Elaris Vaunthel and received the first mission of the campaign.

Known market notes: [Everlund Shops](everlund_shops.md)

#### Moongleam Tower

The Everlund location connected to Elaris. The party received lodging there before leaving for the Silent Coin Tavern.

#### The Moonwood Road

The route north toward the Silent Coin Tavern. The party encountered unnatural wolf behavior during the journey.

#### Roadside Clearing

A familiar stopping place for Tovin that became the site of the party's first fight together. Wolves circled the clearing in a strange territorial pattern.

#### Silent Coin Tavern

An abandoned roadside tavern in the Moonwood. It was aware, rule-bound, and dangerous. Once the party entered, the outside world vanished from sight and sound.

The party defeated the final guardian around the Coiled Coin and escaped through a hidden river passage as the tavern collapsed. From outside, three days had passed while the party experienced only a few hours inside.

Known areas so far:

| Area | What Happened |
|---|---|
| Common Room | The ledger opened, the house claimed the party, mimics attacked, and the first shard was found beneath the hearth. |
| Second Floor | Furniture, rugs, plates, and unseen force attacked the party along the balcony and nearby rooms. |
| Third Floor Guest Rooms | The rooms showed signs of trapped guests who could see the road but could not leave. The second shard was recovered from Room 7. |
| Fourth Floor Records | Ledgers, debt cabinets, the Unsettled Account Box, and Oren's hidden records revealed the Coiled Coin's name and history. |
| Attic Hoard | Guest belongings gathered into a hoard around Blackpenny's echo. The third shard was recovered there. |
| Basement | An old cell, warnings, final shard, and hidden altar forces led to the Coiled Coin reforming and the guardian appearing. |

### Arc 2 - Broken Banner / Redmark Keep

#### Brindleford Road

The back-road route from Everlund toward Brindleford. Elaris recommended it as safer than the main road after reports of caravan attacks near Redmark Keep.

Known signs so far:

| Sign | What The Party Found |
|---|---|
| Red cloth markers | Strips tied to branches, possibly marking territory or claims. |
| Caravan raid site | Torn supplies, scattered trade goods, and signs of violence. |
| Gnoll-like tracks | Rougher tracks connected to the night attack on the party's camp. |
| Disciplined boot tracks | Organized marching signs separate from the gnoll tracks. |

#### Roadside Ruin Camp

An old ruin off the back road where the party made camp. The partial walls and careful setup helped prevent the gnolls from catching the party by surprise. The camp remains the current location as coordinated movement begins closing in around it.

#### Brindleford

A settlement near Redmark Keep. The party has not reached it yet, but Elaris gave them a letter of introduction for a local warden there.

#### Redmark Keep

An old ruin near Brindleford. Caravans have been attacked nearby, supplies have gone missing, and reports from the area have grown stranger and more violent.
