## Everlund

The city where the party met Elaris Vaunthel and received the first mission of the campaign.

Known market notes: [Everlund Shops](everlund_shops.md)

## Moongleam Tower

Elaris Vaunthel's tower in Everlund. The party received lodging and mission briefings there, returned after escaping the Silent Coin Tavern, and left the recovered Coiled Coin in Elaris's care for controlled study.

## Silent Coin Tavern

An abandoned roadside tavern in the Moonwood. It was aware, rule-bound, and dangerous. Once the party entered, the outside world vanished from sight and sound.

The party recovered the four pieces of the Coiled Coin, defeated the tavern's final guardian, and escaped through a hidden river passage as the building collapsed. Three days passed outside while the party experienced only a few hours within.

## Brindleford

A settlement near Redmark Keep. The party has not reached it yet, but Elaris gave them a letter of introduction for a local warden there. A Red Banner captain described the town as under siege and treated any travelers bound for it as possible suppliers or spies.

## Redmark Keep

The party's Arc 2 destination: an old ruin near Brindleford connected to caravan attacks, missing supplies, gnoll violence, and disciplined Red Banner activity.

Further information is currently unknown.
